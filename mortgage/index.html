Frequently Asked Questions

Where do the leads come from? ▼

Our system scrapes county clerk and county recorder databases across all 67 Florida counties. We pull new mortgage recordings, deed transfers, foreclosure filings, building permits, business registrations, and divorce filings daily. The data is public record.

How fresh are the leads? ▼

Our pipeline runs every morning. Most leads are 1-3 days from the original recording date. You reach borrowers while the transaction is still fresh, not weeks later when they have already been contacted by competitors.

Are the leads exclusive? ▼

On the Pro plan, each lead is sold to a maximum of one broker. Growth and Starter plans may share leads with a limited number of other brokers in the same county.

What mortgage opportunities do these leads represent? ▼

New mortgage recordings show recent borrowers who may want to refinance. Property purchases show new homeowners who need mortgage protection. Divorce filings indicate refinance needs. New businesses need commercial mortgages. Construction permits signal construction loan opportunities. Foreclosures present short sale and loan modification leads.

Can I filter by county? ▼

Yes. During signup you select your county preference. You can also filter leads by county, lead type, and recording date from within the dashboard.

Is there a long-term contract? ▼

No. All plans are month-to-month. Cancel anytime from your dashboard. No cancellation fees, no lock-in periods.
