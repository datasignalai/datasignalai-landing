Frequently Asked Questions

Where do the leads come from? ▼

Our AI scans dozens of official Florida government databases and public record sources every day. We monitor probate filings, property sales, marriage licenses, mortgage records, business registrations, building permits, and code violations. The data comes directly from state and county government systems.

How is this different from Zillow or BoldLeads? ▼

Zillow and BoldLeads sell internet leads from people clicking ads. Those leads are sold to 5-8 agents at $25-50 each. Our leads are sourced from government records and based on real life events. When someone inherits a property or files for a marriage license, they have a real need. You're the first to call, not the eighth.

What types of agents is this for? ▼

Listing agents: Probate, code violations, distressed properties. Buyer's agents: Newlyweds, mortgage pre-approvals. Commercial agents: New business filings. Investor-focused agents: Multi-property owners, property sales. Works for solo agents, teams, and brokerages.

How fresh are the leads? ▼

Our pipeline runs every morning. Most leads are 1-3 days old from the original filing date. You're contacting people during their decision-making window, not weeks or months after the fact.

Are the leads exclusive to me? ▼

Exclusivity depends on your plan. Full Territory: Exclusive lock. You are the only agent in your counties. Buyer Pipeline: Semi-exclusive, limited to 3 agents per county. Listing Starter: Shared leads (up to 5 agents).
