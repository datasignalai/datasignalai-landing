Frequently Asked Questions

Where do the leads come from? ▼

Our system pulls new business filings, annual reports, dissolutions, and reinstatements from Florida Sunbiz (Division of Corporations) and county clerk databases. The data is public record.

How fresh are the leads? ▼

Our pipeline runs every morning. Most leads are 1-3 days from the original filing date. You are reaching new business owners while they are actively setting up operations and looking for professional help.

Are the leads exclusive? ▼

On the Pro plan, each lead is sold to a maximum of one accountant/CPA. Growth and Starter plans may share leads with a limited number of other accounting professionals in the same county.

What contact information is included? ▼

Each lead includes business name, entity type, officer/member names, registered agent, mailing address, principal address, and filing date. Phone and email are included when available from supplemental public records.

Is there a long-term contract? ▼

No. All plans are month-to-month. Cancel anytime from your dashboard. No cancellation fees, no lock-in periods.

Can I filter by county or entity type? ▼

Yes. During signup you select your county preference. You can also filter leads by county, entity type, filing type, and date from within the dashboard.
