Frequently Asked Questions

Where do the leads come from? ▼

Our AI scans dozens of official Florida government databases and public record sources every day. We monitor new business registrations, property transactions, professional license filings, permit applications, and other life events that create insurance needs. The data comes directly from state and county government systems.

How fresh are the leads? ▼

Our pipeline runs every morning. Most leads are 1-3 days old from the original filing date. Some sources update within 24 hours. You're contacting people during their decision-making window, not weeks or months after the fact.

Are the leads exclusive to me? ▼

Exclusivity depends on your plan. Pro plan: Exclusive leads. Each lead is sent only to you. Growth plan: Semi-exclusive. Each lead is shared with a maximum of 3 agents. Starter plan: Shared leads (up to 5 agents per lead). All plans cover any FL county you choose.

What contact info is included? ▼

Each lead includes: name, company (if business), address, city, county, and ZIP. Phone and email are included when publicly available. Business filings typically include registered agent contact information. Coverage varies by source. We also provide AI lead scoring, insurance category tags, and ready-to-use call scripts with every lead.

How is this different from buying leads on the open market? ▼
