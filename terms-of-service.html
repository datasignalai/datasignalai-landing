7a. Scope of Exclusivity (Per-Category Rule)
Exclusivity on all plans is scoped per insurance category, not per person. Some prospects appear in our database in more than one capacity. For example, a business owner may appear once as a business lead for commercial coverage and separately as a personal lead for life, umbrella, homeowners, or personal auto coverage. These are treated as distinct lead records with distinct insurance categories. Exclusive and semi-exclusive plans guarantee that no other subscriber will receive the same lead record for the same insurance category. They do not guarantee that the same individual will never be contacted by another agent selling a different, non-competing line of insurance. To reduce same-day double outreach, once either record in a paired business and personal set is delivered, the other record is suppressed from our delivery pool for 48 hours.
