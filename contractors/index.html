Frequently Asked Questions

Where do the leads come from? ▼

Our system pulls building permits, code violations, and project filings from county building departments across all 67 Florida counties. The data is public record.

How fresh are the leads? ▼

Our pipeline runs every morning. Most leads are 1-3 days from the permit issue date. You are reaching homeowners while they are actively looking for contractors, not after they have already hired someone.

Are the leads exclusive? ▼

On the Pro plan, each lead is sold to a maximum of one contractor. Growth and Starter plans may share leads with a limited number of other contractors in the same county.

What contact information is included? ▼

Each lead includes property owner names, mailing addresses, permit property address, permit type, project value, and issue date. Phone and email are included when available from supplemental public records.

Is there a long-term contract? ▼

No. All plans are month-to-month. Cancel anytime from your dashboard. No cancellation fees, no lock-in periods.

Can I filter by county or trade? ▼

Yes. During signup you select your county and trade specialty. You can also filter leads by county, permit type, and project value from within the dashboard.
