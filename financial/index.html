Frequently Asked Questions

Where do the leads come from? ▼

Our system pulls trust formations, probate filings, property records, guardianship petitions, and business filings from Florida county clerks, property appraisers, and Sunbiz. The data is public record.

How fresh are the leads? ▼

Our pipeline runs every morning. Most leads are 1-3 days from the original filing date. You are reaching people during their decision window, not weeks after they have already engaged another advisor.

Are the leads exclusive? ▼

On the Pro plan, each lead is sold to a maximum of one advisor. Growth and Starter plans may share leads with a limited number of other advisors in the same county.

What contact information is included? ▼

Each lead includes names, mailing addresses, filing type, filing date, and county. Phone and email are included when available from supplemental public records.

Is there a long-term contract? ▼

No. All plans are month-to-month. Cancel anytime from your dashboard. No cancellation fees, no lock-in periods.

Can I filter by county? ▼

Yes. During signup you select your county preference. You can also filter leads by county, filing type, and date from within the dashboard.
