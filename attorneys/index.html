Frequently Asked Questions

Where do the leads come from? ▼

Our system scrapes county clerk of court databases across all 67 Florida counties. We pull new case filings daily from traffic, civil, criminal, family, and probate court dockets. The data is public record.

How fresh are the leads? ▼

Our pipeline runs every morning. Most leads are 1-3 days from the original filing date. You are reaching potential clients during their decision window, not weeks later when they have already retained counsel.

Are the leads exclusive? ▼

On the Pro plan, each lead is sold to a maximum of one attorney. Growth and Starter plans may share leads with a limited number of other attorneys in the same county.

What contact information is included? ▼

Each lead includes party names, mailing addresses on file with the court, case number, filing date, case type, and court division. Phone and email are included when available from supplemental public records.

Is there a long-term contract? ▼

No. All plans are month-to-month. Cancel anytime from your dashboard. No cancellation fees, no lock-in periods.

Can I filter by county? ▼

Yes. During signup you select your county preference. You can also filter leads by county, case type, and filing date from within the dashboard.
